## Brand & Style

Orka'i ships two complementary themes tuned for infrastructure management and developer tools. Both prioritize high-density information architecture, immediate cognitive clarity, and a technical, utilitarian personality.

**Orka'i Dark** follows **Deep Ocean Minimalism** — an immersive, precise command-center aesthetic. Technical Minimalism with subtle Glassmorphism: strict structural grids, 1px high-contrast borders, and deep blue-saturated transparency create depth without clutter. Surfaces read as layers of water, growing more luminous as they rise in the hierarchy.

**Orka'i Utility Design** (light) is **Utility-First Minimalism** — stable, controlled, and clean. A monochrome base accented by Ocean Teal, with flat high-contrast surfaces and strategic backdrop blur in navigation. A subtle background grid evokes structural engineering and precision.

## Colors

### Dark — Deep Ocean

The palette is rooted in a deep, blue-saturated black (`#0c1324`) for more visual interest than standard charcoal.

- **Background (Lowest):** Main application canvas (`surface-container-lowest`).
- **Surface (Low):** Sidebars, secondary navigation, and base card layers (`surface-container-low`).
- **Surface (High):** Elevated elements — modals, popovers, active state indicators (`surface-container-high`).
- **Ocean Teal (`#0E7490`):** Primary action color. Use for primary buttons and critical paths on dark backgrounds.
- **Vivid Sky (`#81d1f0` / `#7bd0ff`):** Technical accents, icons, and status indicators — use sparingly for legibility against the deep blue base.
- **Borders:** Semi-transparent blue tints rather than solid grays to maintain tonal consistency.

### Light — Utility

The palette is rooted in a professional **monochrome base** (Primary `#0F1720`) accented by **Ocean Teal** (`#0E7490`) for primary actions and brand presence.

- **Primary & Tertiary:** High-contrast text and structural elements (sidebar) to establish hierarchy.
- **Secondary (Ocean Teal):** Functional accent for buttons, progress bars, and active states.
- **Surface Strategy:** Background `#F8FBFC` with a subtle grid; containers use pure white (`#FFFFFF`) to pop against the background.
- **Semantic Colors:** Green (`#16A34A`) and Amber (`#CA8A04`) strictly for status indicators, using low-saturation background tints to prevent visual fatigue.

## Typography

### Dark

Balances the approachability of **Inter** with the precision of **Geist** and **Geist Mono** (Note: the entire Authentication/Login page experience uses **Geist Mono** exclusively for a pure technical, hyper-minimalist entrance).

- **Geist:** Headlines — sharp, modern technical feel.
- **Inter:** Body copy — high readability during long sessions.
- **Geist Mono:** Dev Mode details — metadata, timestamps, configuration values, and small-scale labels.

For mobile, scale `headline-xl` down to `32px` and `headline-lg` to `24px`.

### Light

**Geist** for all UI elements; **Geist Mono** for metadata, versioning, resource metrics, and the entire Authentication/Login page experience.

- **Headlines:** Tight letter spacing, medium-to-semibold weights.
- **Data Display:** Numerical data uses `display` or `code` roles depending on context.
- **Labels:** Small, uppercase-ready labels for metadata headers within cards.

## Layout & Spacing

Both themes use a **12-column fluid grid** on desktop and a **4-column grid** on mobile, driven by a **4px baseline**.

### Dark

High information density. Component padding is compact (8px or 12px) to maximize visible data.

- **Gaps:** Use `1px` gaps with border colors for bento-box layouts.
- **Sidebars:** Fixed at `240px` or `280px` depending on content complexity.
- **Technical Insets:** 16px internal padding for most containers.

### Light

**Fixed-Fluid hybrid:** Fixed-width sidebar (`240px`) anchors the left; main content caps at `max-w-7xl` (1280px).

- **Grid:** Bento-style summary cards span 4 columns each on desktop; workload cards span 4 or 6 columns.
- **Rhythm:** Standard component internal padding is 12px (`md`) or 16px (`lg`).
- **Mobile:** Sidebar collapses to a hamburger menu; grid columns stack with 16px side margins.

## Elevation & Depth

Depth is communicated through **Tonal Layering** rather than aggressive shadows in both themes.

### Dark

1. **Z-0 (Background):** `#0c1324` / `#070d1f`
2. **Z-1 (Plates):** `#151b2d` with a `1px` border of `rgba(56, 189, 248, 0.1)`
3. **Z-2 (Interactive):** `#23293c`
4. **Glass Effect:** Floating menus — `backdrop-blur` (12px–20px) + semi-transparent fill (`rgba(7, 13, 31, 0.8)`)

Avoid drop shadows except for extreme elevation (global modals). Use a large, soft blur with a deep blue tint (`rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.5)`).

### Light

- **Level 0 (Background):** `#F8FBFC` with a 24px light-grey grid pattern.
- **Level 1 (Cards/Containers):** White background, 1px solid border (`#D1DEE2`), subtle `shadow-sm`.
- **Level 2 (Navigation/Floating):** `backdrop-blur` (12px) with 70–90% opacity white backgrounds.
- **Level 3 (Command Palette):** High-contrast dark surfaces (`#233144`) with `shadow-lg`.

## Shapes

Both themes use **Soft Precision** — subdued roundedness that stays technical.

- **Standard Elements:** `4px` (`0.25rem`) radius — buttons, inputs, small chips.
- **Containers:** `8px` (`0.5rem`) radius for cards and main UI blocks.
- **Inner Elements:** Inner radius smaller than outer when nesting (button inside card).
- **Tags/Badges (light):** `2px` radius to differentiate from interactive buttons.
- **Avatars (light):** `4px` radius (square with soft corners), not circular.
- **Dev Mode (dark):** Sharp corners (`0px`) for code blocks and terminal windows.

## Components

### Buttons

**Dark**
- **Primary:** Background `#0E7490`, white text, no border.
- **Secondary:** Transparent background, `1px` border using primary color, text `#38BDF8`.
- **Ghost:** No background, no border, text `#38BDF8`.

**Light**
- **Primary:** Ocean Teal background, white text.
- **Secondary:** Ghost style with `1px` borders.
- Icons within buttons: 14px or 16px.

### Inputs

**Dark:** Background `#151b2d`, `1px` border of outline variant. Focus: border `#38BDF8` with subtle outer glow (`0px 0px 4px`).

**Light:** Background `#FFFFFF`, `1px` border, `4px` radius. Include keyboard shortcut hints (e.g. ⌘K) where applicable.

### Chips / Tags / Badges

**Dark:** Geist Mono, dark background (`#23293c`) with sky-blue text.

**Light:** Semi-transparent semantic color tints (10% opacity) with full-opacity text. Pulse animation for active/healthy states.

### Cards

**Dark:** `surface-container-low` or `surface-container-high`, `1px` border on all sides. Header sections separated by a `1px` horizontal line.

**Light:** White background, `1px` border (`#D1DEE2`), `4px` radius. Content divided by thin, low-opacity separators.

### Data Tables

**Dark:** `1px` dividers. Row hover: `rgba(56, 189, 248, 0.05)`. Headers use `label-caps`.

**Light:** Same divider strategy; headers use `label-md`.

### Progress Bars (light)

Extremely thin (4px height), rounded track, high-contrast fill.

### Sidebar Nav (light)

Dark theme sidebar (`tertiary` / `#000000`) contrasting with light main content. Active states: left-hand border accent in Secondary (Ocean Teal).
